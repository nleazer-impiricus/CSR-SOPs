Detailed SOP Steps

Intake & Ticket Setup

The ticket is created and assigned to you in Zendesk under your unsolved tickets.

  • Conversation ticket — all HCP-facing communication after the initial acknowledgement (public replies enabled)
  • Tracker ticket — call recordings, screenshots, progress summaries (internal notes only)

Send Initial HCP Acknowledgement

Send within 24 hours of ticket creation.

  1. Open the assigned inbound ticket
  2. Switch reply type to Public Reply
  3. Apply approved greeting macro (or custom message) — reference the request, set next-step expectations
  4. Check Send Text
  5. Review and Submit as Responded
⚠️
When the HCP replies, Zendesk creates a new Conversation Ticket. The original becomes the Tracker Ticket (internal notes only from this point).
Sending a text from Tracker Tickets: Check "Send Text" in left-hand window → change Internal Notes to Public Reply → Submit
Sending a text from Conversation Tickets: Change Internal Notes to SMS → Submit

Check the Knowledge Base

Search the KB for a verified sample request process for this product.

🔀Process found in KB?
Yes → Follow documented steps → Update HCP in Conversation Ticket → Submit as Awaiting Response → Log actions in Tracker as internal note → Submit Tracker as In Progress
No / Steps appear incorrect → Alert #concierge-call-center and proceed to Step 4 (Research).

Research & Manufacturer Contact

If no verified KB process exists, begin researching the product.

🤖
AI tools may be used as a starting point. All information must be verified before sending to an HCP. Links must not include an AI source URL.

Within 24 hours of the initial acknowledgement: Contact manufacturer via preferred method (see Request Methods tab).

Update HCP once contact is made or new information is available. See the Request Methods tab for portal, phone, and rep request procedures.

🚩
Any new information found must be flagged to the HCP Coordinators in #concierge-call-center for approval before being shared with the HCP. Once flagged, log that you posted for approval as an internal note in the Tracker Ticket.
📌
Important: Confirm all HCP address information before submitting requests. Store verified details in Zendesk user fields. Always use the Concierge email and your individual Quo line unless the HCP requested otherwise.

Tracker Ticket Logging

Log every action as an internal note in the Tracker Ticket:

  • Form submissions
  • Manufacturer emails or calls (include phone numbers and rep emails used)
  • Any relevant communication

Submit Tracker Tickets as In Progress after manufacturer outreach.

Ongoing Follow-Up & Escalation

  • Follow up with manufacturer/rep every 5–7 days until resolution
  • Send HCP updates at minimum every 5 business days
  • If no manufacturer/rep communication after 3 days → escalate to HCP Coordinators
  • Update Tracker Ticket daily with internal notes while escalated
  • If escalated 3× without resolution → flag to HCP Coordinators to determine if outreach should end

Shipment Confirmation

If shipment or delivery is confirmed:

  • Notify HCP with shipping details or expectations
  • Submit ticket as Awaiting Confirmation

Resolution & Closure

🔀How did this resolve?
Resolved → HCP confirmed samples were delivered or rep visit took place
Done → Samples not available or HCP ineligible (offer rep visit, similar products from KB, or products that treat same condition). Also Done when tracking shows delivered but HCP unresponsive for 7 days or after 2+ follow-up attempts.
💡
When closing as Done due to ineligibility, offer alternatives: rep office visit, similar products from KB that treat the same condition, or products aligned with the HCP's specialty area.

Ticket Statuses

📌
There are two ticket types. Tracker Tickets track your actions and progress (internal notes only). Conversation Tickets are created when an HCP responds and are used for all HCP-facing communication.

🗂 Tracker Ticket Statuses

StatusWhen to Use
NewTicket created but no communication has been sent to the HCP yet.
OpenTicket assigned to you, but you haven't started working on it yet. Change to Responded once you initiate contact.
RespondedIntro message sent to the HCP. You have not yet contacted a rep or manufacturer.
In ProgressManufacturer/rep contacted, you are researching, or otherwise actively working on the ticket. HCP has been updated and told you'll follow up. Includes filled-out sample forms with no shipping confirmation yet.
Awaiting ConfirmationPortal link sent to HCP, samples confirmed shipped, rep info sent, rep said they're reaching out, or rep visit is scheduled. Only waiting for HCP to confirm receipt or visit completion.
On HoldWaiting on information with no reasonable ETA (e.g., 4–6 week registration approval wait, unreleased product). Ask in the channel before setting this status.
ResolvedReceived confirmation from HCP and/or rep that the visit took place or samples were delivered.
DonePortal sent with confirmed shipment or rep notified, but HCP is unresponsive. OR HCP is ineligible per manufacturer guidelines.
DisregardDuplicate ticket or ticket created by accident. Notify Nick and Lauren before disregarding.

💬 Conversation Ticket Statuses

StatusWhat It Means
Responded ⚠️ Action needed. On a Conversation Ticket, "Responded" means the HCP has responded to you. If you see this status on a Conversation Ticket, you need to reach out to the HCP.
Awaiting Response You have responded to the HCP. Set this after sending a message so the status color changes and the team can see you've replied.
No Active Tickets Set when all active tickets for this HCP are completed. Preserves the conversation history so if the HCP reaches out again, the ticket reactivates with full history.

Request Methods (Appendix A)

Use these methods in order of preference when submitting sample or rep requests on behalf of an HCP.

⭐ Preferred: Manufacturer Sample Website / Portal

Always try the manufacturer's official sample portal first.

Information you may need:

  • HCP Name
  • NPI number
  • Credentials (MD, DO, NP, PA, etc.)
  • Specialty
  • Practice Name
  • Practice Address (verify via independent search — no home addresses)
🔐
If a portal account is required: Send the HCP the portal link and let them know that sample availability may vary. Offer support if issues arise.

📝 Alternative: Rep Request / General Inquiry via Manufacturer Website

Submit a rep request or general inquiry form through the manufacturer's website if no direct sample portal exists.

📞 Alternative: Call or Email Customer Support

Use if no web option exists. State clearly that the Concierge is acting on behalf of the HCP/practice.

💬
If asked about your direct relationship to the HCP/practice, you can say you're assisting them with the request or that you are an assistant.

🚨 No Communication After 3 Days

Partner with HCP Coordinators. Update Tracker Ticket daily with internal notes.

Detailed SOP Steps

Fill in Zendesk User Fields

Many fields populate automatically at ticket creation — fill in any missing information:

  • Name & Credentials (MD, DO, NP, PA, etc.) — needed to address them correctly in all future communications
  • Phone number
  • NPI
  • Specialty
  • Practice Name
  • Practice Address
  • License Number
  • Notes: Any other info gathered (fax number, additional practice name/address, etc.)

Send Introductory Text Message

Send within 24 hours of ticket creation using the "Greeting explaining the Concierge service" macro.

📱
From a Tracker Ticket: Check "Send Text" in left-hand window → change Internal Notes to Public Reply → Submit

From a Conversation Ticket: Change Internal Notes to SMS → Submit

Correct any pre-filled information in the macro as needed. You'll be prompted to confirm before the message sends.

Handle HCP Response

🔀What does the HCP want?

Option A: Rep or sample request

→ Flag in #concierge-call-center. HCP Coordinator will update the ticket title or create a new ticket. Follow the Sample & Rep Request SOP.

Option B: HCP wants suggestions

→ Use the Knowledge Base to recommend 2–3 items relevant to their specialty area and practice. If nothing in the KB matches, search for popular products or available provider samples, or use ChatGPT for product suggestions — but clarify with the HCP: "We will look into these product samples" (do not say you can definitely get them).

Always ask the HCP first if they want you to look into the products you find.

Any new information found must be flagged to the HCP Coordinators in #concierge-call-center for approval before being shared with the HCP. Log that you posted for approval as an internal note in the Tracker Ticket.
  • If the HCP wants you to look into a specific product → flag in channel for a new/updated ticket and follow the Sample & Rep Request SOP
  • If the HCP wants to hold off → let them know to reach back out, send the survey link, and submit as Done

Handle No Response

  1. Follow up the next day asking if there's anything they'd like you to get started for them
  2. If still no response the following day, send an inactivity message
  3. Submit as Done
